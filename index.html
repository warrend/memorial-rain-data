About the Site

If you’re a fan of The Memorial, you know the tournament brings two things to the Columbus area: great golf and inclement weather. This site is an attempt, based on historical data from The Memorial’s site, to present weather over the years in a visual way.

Rain Per Tournament

We’ll start by looking at rain per event. Dry tournaments represent an event without rain on any of the four days of play. Rain tournaments represent an event with rain on at least one of the four days.
